Brigadeiro

Ingredients (8 servings)

  • ⚬ 1 tablespoon butter
  • ⚬ 14 oz sweetened condensed milk(395 g)
  • ⚬ Â¼ cup cocoa powder(30 g)
  • ⚬ 1 cup chocolate sprinkle(160 g), as needed

Preparation:

STEP 1 - In a pot over low heat, melt the butter, condensed milk, and cocoa powder, stirring continuously until you can see the bottom of the pot for 2-3 seconds when dragging a spatula through.

STEP 2 - Pour onto a greased plate, then chill for 1 hour.

STEP 3 - Shape and roll the chilled mixture into balls.

STEP 4 - Roll the balls in chocolate sprinkles.
